Common Questions

Honest Answers to Common Questions

Is this cold emailing? Will it hurt my brand?
We use targeted, professional outreach — not spam. Every message is relevant, personalized to the business we're reaching, and compliant with applicable email laws. We protect your brand in every outreach we send.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients begin receiving responses and conversations within the first 2–3 weeks of launch. Volume and quality improve over the first 60 days as we optimize targeting and messaging.
Do I need a CRM or any special software?
No. We can work with what you have — or provide the tools needed as part of your package. The Full System tier includes full CRM setup and integration.
What industries do you work with?
We work across most B2B industries — professional services, agencies, SaaS, manufacturing, logistics, consulting, and more. If your business sells to other businesses, we can build a system for it.
What if it doesn't work for my business?
That's exactly why there are no long-term contracts. If the outreach isn't generating quality conversations, you can stop at any time. We start with a test phase specifically to validate fit before scaling.
How is this different from hiring a salesperson?
A salesperson handles inbound calls and closes. We generate the pipeline — the conversations they need to close. It's not either/or. We build the top of funnel so your team can focus entirely on closing deals.
