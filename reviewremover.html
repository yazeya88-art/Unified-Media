Questions and Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before moving forward.

Can you really guarantee review removal?
We guarantee results on eligible reviews — meaning reviews that violate Google's content policies. We conduct a free audit first to identify exactly which reviews qualify. If a review is not eligible, we tell you upfront and don't charge anything.
Is this legal? Will I get penalized by Google?
Absolutely legal. We exclusively use Google's own policy frameworks and escalation channels. We never purchase fake reviews, spam the platform, or violate terms of service. This is reputation management done right — not black-hat tactics.
What types of reviews can you remove?
We can remove fake reviews, competitor reviews, reviews from non-customers, spam, reviews with prohibited content, reviews that violate Google's name-calling or hate speech policy, and reviews from employees. Our audit identifies every removable review on your profile.
How much does it cost?
We work on a performance basis — you pay only when a review is successfully removed. Pricing depends on the number of reviews and complexity of the cases. We provide a full, transparent quote during your free audit with zero obligation.
What happens if a removed review comes back?
Once a review is removed by Google, it stays removed. In the rare event it returns due to a platform error, we re-file at no additional cost. We also set up monitoring so you are alerted to any new negative reviews the moment they appear.
How quickly will I see results?
Most removals are completed within 7–14 business days. Urgent cases can sometimes be resolved faster. You will have full visibility into case progress through your dashboard throughout the process.
