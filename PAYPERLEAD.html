This Cookie Policy explains how Unified Media Marketing Group ("UMMG") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. By continuing to browse our site, you consent to our use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and understand how you interact with our pages — so we can improve your experience and the effectiveness of our lead generation services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use
Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These include session cookies that allow our contact forms and lead pricing requests to work. Disabling these may prevent parts of the site from functioning.

Analytics Cookies: We use tools such as Google Analytics to track page views, session duration, and traffic sources. This data helps us understand which pages and campaigns are driving the most qualified leads — allowing us to continuously improve our targeting.

Marketing & Retargeting Cookies: We may use advertising pixels (such as Google Ads or Meta) to track conversions and retarget visitors who have shown interest in our pay-per-lead services. These cookies help ensure our ad spend remains efficient and that relevant messaging reaches the right business owners.

Preference Cookies: These remember settings you have applied, such as your preferred lead type or market, to provide a more personalized experience on return visits.

3. Third-Party Cookies
Some cookies on this site are placed by third-party service providers, including analytics platforms, advertising networks, and CRM integrations. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they use the data collected. UMMG does not control third-party cookies and encourages you to review their respective policies.

4. How Long Do Cookies Last?
Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies may remain on your device for up to 24 months, depending on their purpose. Marketing and retargeting cookies typically last 30–90 days.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences
You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, or be notified before a cookie is set. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our site, including the ability to submit lead pricing requests or use our contact forms.

6. Do Not Track
Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. At this time, UMMG does not alter its data collection practices in response to DNT signals, as there is no universal standard for how these should be interpreted. We remain committed to transparency about how data is collected.
